01 — Executive Summary

What Is OptiCapture?

OptiCapture is a web-based inventory management platform built for small and mid-sized businesses that have outgrown spreadsheets but don't need the cost or complexity of enterprise ERP systems. It transforms the physical act of counting and recording stock into a fast, accurate, mobile-driven workflow — replacing clipboards, Excel files, and manual data entry with barcode scanning from any smartphone.

Any phone. Any browser. No app install required. Point, scan, done — inventory updated in real time.

Designed for convenience stores, grocery retailers, warehouses, and any operation where staff walk the floor counting stock, OptiCapture provides the tools to scan, review, audit, and commit inventory — all from a single dashboard that works equally well on a desktop and a phone.

The Problem We're Solving

📋

Manual Counting — Human Error

Staff walk aisles with clipboards or phones, writing counts by hand. Misreads, skipped items, and transcription errors are inevitable and often invisible until it's too late.

📊

Excel as a Database — Not Built for This

Spreadsheets weren't designed for multi-person live inventory. Conflicting edits, no audit trail, no barcode integration, and no real-time visibility across staff.

🐢

Slow Turnaround

A full inventory count can take days to complete, collate, and upload. By the time the data is clean, it's already out of date.

💸

Legacy Systems Are Expensive

Existing platforms like Petrosoft charge high monthly fees, require proprietary hardware, and offer limited flexibility for modern mobile workflows.

02 — Capabilities

What OptiCapture Provides

📱

Mobile Barcode Scanning

Any smartphone camera becomes a barcode scanner. Staff scan a QR code to link their phone to a live session — no app download, no hardware purchase.

🖥️

Real-Time Desktop Feed

The owner's desktop shows every scan as it happens. Multiple staff scanning simultaneously — each item appears instantly with product name, image, and status.

📝

Draft Sessions

Scans are held in a draft before committing. Owners review, edit quantities, flag unknowns, and selectively commit — nothing hits the live inventory until approved.

👥

Role-Based Access

Owners control, approve, and commit. Takers scan and save drafts. Permissions enforced at the server level — takers cannot modify committed inventory.

🔍

Auto Product Lookup

Each barcode is automatically matched to a product name, brand, and image via external databases. Unknown items are flagged for manual review.

📁

Import & Export

Bulk-import existing inventory from Excel or CSV on day one. Export back to Excel, CSV, JSON, or PDF at any time — full interoperability with existing tools.

🗂️

Category Management

Organise inventory into custom categories. Scanned items are assigned to categories on commit, keeping stock structured and searchable.

📜

Full Audit Trail

Every scan is logged — what was scanned, when, by whom, and what session it belonged to. Scan history is preserved even after sessions are closed.

1
Available Now

File-Based Integration

Import existing Excel/CSV inventory on day one. Staff scan with phones, owner reviews and commits. Export back to Excel or CSV after each count. Zero technical setup — works with any system that reads spreadsheets. Enough to run a full inventory operation from day one.

2
Next

API Access + Premium Lookups

API keys enable machine-to-machine auth — the business's POS or custom system can call OptiCapture directly to read inventory without a browser session. Premium product lookup APIs (GoUPC, Barcodelookup) unlock richer product images and descriptions per barcode. Cloud backup and notification alerts also ship in this phase.

Near Future

When inventory is committed, OptiCapture fires a webhook to notify external systems automatically — no manual exports. QuickBooks and Xero receive inventory value updates. Shopify and WooCommerce stock levels update in real time. No developer needed on the business side once configured.

One-click "Connect to Square" or "Connect to Clover" buttons in Store Settings. A Zapier connector opens OptiCapture to 5,000+ third-party apps with no code. Full bidirectional sync with major POS systems. Franchise and multi-location management with centralised reporting.

07 — Roadmap

Planned Improvements

Premium Product Lookup APIs

Replace free-tier lookups with GoUPC or Barcodelookup — significantly better image quality, more accurate descriptions, and broader retail barcode coverage. Offered as a per-store premium upgrade.

Reporting & Analytics Dashboard

Inventory value over time, scan velocity by taker, category breakdowns, and stock movement reports. Visual charts for management-level review.

Low Stock Alerts

Configurable minimum stock thresholds per item or category. Automatic email or SMS notification when stock falls below threshold — proactive reordering.

UI Refinements

Ongoing improvements based on real-world usage feedback — mobile scan interface, dashboard layout, item card design, and accessibility improvements.

Database Upgrade Path

Migration path from SQLite to PostgreSQL for high-volume stores or multi-location deployments requiring concurrent write performance.

Multi-Location Management

Centralised dashboard for franchise or chain operations — view inventory across all locations, transfer stock between stores, unified reporting.

Offline Mode

Queue scans locally when network drops during a scan session. Sync to server automatically when reconnected — no lost data in low-connectivity environments.

Supplier Purchase Orders

Auto-generate purchase orders from committed inventory counts. Send directly to supplier portals or export as PDF for manual submission.
